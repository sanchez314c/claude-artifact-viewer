# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Be respectful. Be constructive. Focus on the work.

Specifically:
- No harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Critique code, not people
- Accept feedback without defensiveness
- Help newcomers get started

## Scope

This applies to all project spaces: issues, pull requests, and any other communication related to this project.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, open a private issue or contact the maintainer directly via GitHub.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this standard. Repeated violations may result in a permanent ban from the project.
